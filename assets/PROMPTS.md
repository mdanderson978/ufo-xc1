# UFO: XC1 — AI image prompt pack

Generate these with your AI image subscription whenever convenient, then drop
each result into `assets/incoming/<ID>/` (e.g. `assets/incoming/P001/image.png`).
Nothing blocks on these — every slot has a procedural placeholder until its
image arrives. Prefer PNG. Match the requested aspect/size where possible;
exact pixel counts don't matter, I resize on integration.
